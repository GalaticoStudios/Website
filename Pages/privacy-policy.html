Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use cookies or any other technology to track your browsing activity on our website. The only thing we store in your browser is a single preference - your choice from the cookie notice banner (accepted or declined) - saved using your browser's local storage so we don't show you the banner again. This is never sent to us or any third party, and you can clear it at any time via your browser's site settings.

Contact

If you email us at inquiries@galaticostudios.co.uk, the information you provide is sent directly to our company email address and retained securely only for the purpose of responding to your enquiry. You may request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us, and we will comply with your request promptly.
