Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 March 2026

Welcome to Galatico Studios Ltd. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our website, play our games, interact with our services, or participate in any competitions or events we run.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our players. This includes:

  • Information You Provide: Account registration details (username, email), profile information, any communications you send to our support team, and email addresses collected at events or competitions.
  • Automated Collection: When you use our site, we may collect your IP address, device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Gameplay Data: Information about your progress, achievements, and interactions within our games.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic.

2. How We Use Your Information

Galatico Studios Ltd uses the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our games and website.
  • To process transactions and send related information (e.g., in-game purchases).
  • To improve our products through analytics and player feedback.
  • To protect against fraudulent or illegal activity.
  • To communicate with you about updates, promotions, and news.
  • To contact you about competitions, prizes, and promotional offers where you have given consent.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help us with hosting, analytics, and payment processing.
  • Marketing Partners: Where you have explicitly consented, we may share your email address with selected third-party marketing partners, including Google and Meta, for marketing purposes. These partners operate under their own privacy policies and are responsible for their own data processing.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or sale, your data may be transferred to the new owner.

4. Your Rights and Choices

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we fix inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data ("the right to be forgotten").
  • Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent (e.g., marketing emails or third-party sharing), you may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us at msmith@galaticostudios.co.uk or using the "unsubscribe" link in any email we send.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the "unsubscribe" link.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it immediately.
